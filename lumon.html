Designing with edits, not guesses [v1.8.0].

This editor treats AI suggestions as a stack of deliberate changes. Instead of replacing an entire paragraph, the assistant can make several visible edits that can be reviewed individually.

Multi-edit mode keeps the human in control: inspect, accept, reject, refine.

What changes here

  • AI suggestions are grouped into multiple precise operations.
  • Each operation has a type: replace, insert, remove, or format.
  • The preview shows every proposed change before it touches the final text.
  • The editor stays lightweight, dark, and focused.
